Our Story

Zazzies™ was conceived by a team of brilliant advertising profesionals in GE Corporate Marketing headquarters.

One year later, an extremely bored intern in Lisle, IL was determined to make Zazzies™ a reality.


Zazzies™ is an interactive cat-based website built to demonstrate the power of the Predix cloud platform.

No cats were harmed in the making of this website.




Zazzies™ is a wholly owned subsidiary of GE Digital SmartSignal and designed specifically for use on the Predix cloud platform.
